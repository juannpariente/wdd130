The Storm Surge: Is an intermediate-level rafting experience designed for those who already have some water experience and are looking for a moderate challenge. On this trip, you'll navigate class III and IV rapids, offering the perfect mix of adrenaline and control. The route takes you through stunning landscapes, surrounded by towering mountains and lush vegetation, as you conquer turbulent waters that will test your rafting skills.

Wild Rapids Expedition: Is the ultimate rafting adventure for seasoned enthusiasts. If you're looking for a truly challenging experience, this advanced-level trip is perfect for you. With Class V rapids, this journey is a true test of endurance and skill. Throughout the course, you'll face furious, challenging waters while navigating deep canyons and rugged landscapes. This trip is for those who crave extreme thrills and a deep connection with untamed nature.

Cascade Rush Adventure: Is the perfect introduction to the world of rafting, designed for beginners or those looking for a more relaxed experience. On this trip, you'll enjoy gentle and calm rapids, allowing you to experience the thrill of rafting without feeling overwhelmed. It's ideal for groups, families, or individuals who have never tried the sport before, and it's a great way to immerse yourself in the beauty of the water landscapes without a heavy physical or technical load.
